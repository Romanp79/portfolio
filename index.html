The Fake Document Detection System is a security-focused solution developed to verify the authenticity of digital documents and detect any form of tampering or manipulation. It uses hashing techniques combined with pattern analysis to ensure document integrity.

The system compares original and modified documents to identify inconsistencies and potential fraud. It is designed to address real-world challenges related to document verification and digital trust.

This project showcases strong knowledge of cybersecurity principles, hashing algorithms, and data validation mechanisms. It reflects the ability to build secure systems that enhance trust and reliability in digital environments.
